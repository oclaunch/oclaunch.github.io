Startup Companies 2016

These are the existing startup companies in OC Launch. To add your company to the list, contact us.

SchoolSmart

SchoolSmart provides a mobile app for schools that allows the teachers to notify parents about school activities and happenings. Parents can use the app to view their child’s grades and assignments, as well as any important information the school needs to convey including important events and dates.

EventsNow

Our team will develop and launch an app called EventsNow that will serve as a database of all the events that are taking place in a neighborhood. Customers will be able to readily access information about events in their area based on interests and time constraints. Vendors, businesses, and other organizations will be able to advertise their events to a larger audience through this app.

Peer Genius

Peer Genius is a peer tutoring app created for students who seek help from their peers. Because the peer tutoring clubs at high schools are often not well run, low in technology, and fragmented, we have created an application where students no longer need to rely on the peer tutoring clubs or expensive professionals. The beauty of Peer Genius is that students do not have to choose to be a tutor or a tutee; instead they can be both. Overall, the goal of Peer Genius is to provide free, quality education to students who need help on their studies.

Ballet for Disability

Many disadvantaged children love to dance but do not have enough opportunities for quality ballet education. I have decided to send a program to other ballet schools around my area so disadvantaged children have the opportunity to learn ballet.
