Who are we?

OC Launch is a High School Entrepreneurship program for Orange County under Math for Service. Join a team of high school students to create a company, meet new people, and learn new skills along the way.

Create a Company

Apply to a company you would like to join, or start your own. Start with an idea and slowly develop it into a working product that you can sell.

Grow your Business

Gradually build your company with your team, and meet new people and learn new things along the way. Learn new skills like marketing, finance, coding, and more along the way.

Win

Compete in nationwide entrepreneurship competitions. Pitch to investors, sell more units, and expand your userbase to win huge prizes at competitions.

Faq

Questions and Answers.

How can I apply?

Applications for joining an existing team will open on December 22nd, 2016. If you are interested in starting a team and leading it, contact us at oclaunchx@gmail.com.

What skills are required to join?

To join a team, much experience is not required. All you need is a little bit of expeirence in coding, marketing, financing, or other business skills.

How much time will I need to put in?

Only the time you are willing to put in. You will be running your business and it is up to you how much time you want to put into it.

What are the benefits of joining?

In OC Launch, you will not only learn valuable skills like marketing and financing that will be useful in the future, but you will get the oppurtunity to meet new people and be a part of the future.

How do I join?

Applications for joining a team open on December 22, 2016. If you want to start your own business, contact us at oclaunchx@gmail.com. Click here to check out the existing companies and team leaders.
